0.46% conviction rate: Of 66,711 women who reached a crisis centre (2001–Jul 2024), only 310 resulted in conviction. The government has never published an official rate.

02

Zero convictions Jan–Jul 2024: 643 women sought help. 227 cases filed. 12 verdicts. Not one conviction.

+52% surge in 2025: Bangladesh Police recorded 786 rape victims H1 2025 vs 516 the year before. Child rape up 75%.

04

10 data voids: The government holds 25 years of conviction data by tribunal. It has never published it. The absence of data is a feature of the system, not a limitation of research.
