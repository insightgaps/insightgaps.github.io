The Lead Belt

44 formally assessed lead-contaminated sites across Bangladesh have at least one school within 500 metres — placing an estimated 39,875 students in proximity to sites where soil lead has been measured at up to 1,702 times the international safety standard. The closest school sits 59 metres from an active recycling site in Kamrangir Char. None have a public record of permanent closure.

The Impunity Machine

Bangladesh rape justice system: 310 convictions from 66,711 women across 23 years of One-Stop Crisis Centre records — a 0.46% conviction rate. The system convicts women who report faster than it convicts the men they report against. In H1 2025: 786 reported cases, up 52%. No official conviction registry existed. We built the first one from primary sources.
