Data Publication Policy

Open Access
All public datasets are freely available. No registration, no paywall.
Anonymization
All identifying information is removed. No individuals or organizations are identifiable.
Disclosure
Where live data cannot be published, methodology and source classifications are documented openly.
Reproducibility
All findings must be traceable to documented, replicable data sources. Challenge the data.
